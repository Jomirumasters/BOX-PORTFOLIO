Hi, I'm Jomiruu

The Enthusiastic Guy

A Video Editor, Graphic Designer, Content Creator, and Motion Graphics Artist. I absolutely love what I do. It's been years since I started dreaming of becoming a freelance editor. It's been a tough process, but I finally figured out how to keep going.

With years of practice and experience, I’ve learned to use software like Adobe Photoshop, After Effects, and Illustrator by watching tutorials on YouTube, Skillshare, and Patreon. From being a perfectionist to learning to work independently, I can now take on more projects. Practice, experience, and discipline make everything fall into place. Every 1% matters!
